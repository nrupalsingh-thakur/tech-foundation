## 09 — Latency vs Bandwidth (Why “Fast” Is Vague)

### Latency

- Time for data to travel
- Measured in milliseconds

**Mental model:**  
“How long until the first byte shows up?”

---

### Bandwidth

- Amount of data per second
- Measured in Mbps / Gbps

**Mental model:**  
“How wide is the pipe?”

High latency, high bandwidth:
slow start, fast transfer

Low latency, low bandwidth:
quick start, slow transfer

Small requests care about latency.  
Large transfers care about bandwidth.
